Spaghetti Bolegnese

Rich bolegnese served on top spaghetti.

An italian classic, juicy meat served in a rich tomatoey sauce served on top of spaghetti. Can be served with garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 medium onions
  • 500g beef mince
  • 800g plum tomatoes
  • 1 beef stock cube

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan on medium heat.
  2. Add onions and mince and cook until the beef is browned.
  3. Add tomatoes and stock cube.
  4. Cook spaghetti and serve.
